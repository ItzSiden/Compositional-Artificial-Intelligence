## About Zylotech
Zylotech Corporation is a fictional internal company used for MSCP testing.
Founded in 2024 by CEO Mira Voss and CTO Dalen Okafor.
Headquarters: Tower 9, Sector 7, Neon Bay, Archipelago Islands.
Employee count: 847 staff across 3 continents.

## Products

### ZyloPad X9
- Our flagship tablet released March 2026
- 14-inch foldable OLED display with 240Hz refresh rate
- Powered by the ZyloChip A3 processor (12-core, 5nm)
- Battery: 9800mAh, charges to 100% in 18 minutes via MagFlow charging
- Available in colors: Obsidian Black, Ghost White, Coral Dusk
- Price: $1,299 USD
- Known issue: ZyloPad X9 units manufactured before serial number ZX9-004500 have a screen flicker bug when brightness is set below 20% — fix is to update firmware to v2.1.3

### ZyloWatch Ultra
- Smartwatch released January 2026
- Circular 1.8-inch sapphire display
- Health features: blood glucose monitoring (no finger prick), sleep apnea detection, hydration sensor
- Battery lasts 11 days on a single charge
- Strap size: 22mm universal
- Price: $449 USD
- Pairs with ZyloPad and ZyloPhone via ZyloSync protocol

### ZyloPhone 15 Pro
- Flagship smartphone, released February 2026
- 6.9-inch Super Retina display, 120Hz ProMotion
- Triple camera: 200MP main, 48MP ultrawide, 12MP periscope zoom (10x optical)
- ZyloChip A3 processor, 16GB RAM, available in 256GB / 512GB / 1TB
- Price: $1,099 (256GB), $1,299 (512GB), $1,599 (1TB)
- Exclusive feature: HoloView mode — projects a small 3D hologram 4cm above the screen using structured light array
- Colors: Midnight, Starlight, Pacific Blue, Titanium Red

## Internal Systems

### ZyloOS 3.0
- Zylotech's proprietary operating system used across all devices
- Based on a modified Android 16 kernel
- Key feature: Adaptive AI Shell — the UI rearranges itself based on time of day and user habits
- App store: ZyloMarket, contains 180,000 apps as of 2026

### ZyloCloud
- Internal cloud platform hosted on Archipelago Islands data centers
- Storage plans: 100GB free, 1TB ($4.99/mo), 10TB ($19.99/mo)
- ZyloCloud Drive syncs across all Zylotech devices in real time
- API endpoint: api.zylocloud.internal (internal network only)

## Support & Troubleshooting
- Support line: 1-800-ZYL-TECH (Mon-Fri, 8am-8pm EST)
- ZyloPad X9 screen flicker: update to firmware v2.1.3
- ZyloWatch Ultra not syncing: toggle ZyloSync off/on in settings, ensure both devices are on ZyloOS 3.0+
- ZyloPhone 15 Pro HoloView not working: HoloView requires minimum 40% battery and is disabled in temperatures below 10°C
